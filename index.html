🕐 Game Format & Timing

1
Formats available: 3v3 and 4v4. Players select their format at signup. 4v4 is the default — if one or both teams are short, the game drops to 3v3.
2
Game structure: All games are played in 2 halves of 10 minutes each. 3-minute halftime break between halves.
3
Shot clock: Each possession is 32 seconds. Failure to attempt a shot before the clock expires results in a turnover.
4
Tiebreaker — Shootout: If the score is tied at the end of regulation, every player (active + sub) shoots one shot each from beyond the arc. Team with the most makes wins. 3v3 = 4 shooters per team 4v4 = 5 shooters per team — If still tied: sudden death — one shooter each, alternating, until one makes and the other misses.

⚖️ Foul System

1
Free foul calls: Each player is granted 3 free foul calls per game. These are accepted without challenge — no questions asked.
2
Challenge threshold: Once a player has used all 3 free foul calls, any foul call from the 4th foul onward may be challenged by the defensive player.
3
How a challenge works: If the defensive player (Player B) disagrees with the foul called by the offensive player (Player A), Player B may declare a challenge. Player B then selects any one player from Player A's team to attempt a 3-point shot from behind the arc.
4
Challenge — Shot made: If the selected shooter makes the 3-pointer, the challenge fails. Player A's team retains possession. The shot clock resets to whichever is greater: the remaining time on the clock or a minimum reset of 20 seconds.
Example: foul called at 21 sec → keep 21 sec. Foul called at 3 sec → reset to 20 sec.
5
Challenge — Shot missed: If the selected shooter misses the 3-pointer, the challenge succeeds. The foul call is overturned. Player B's team gains possession with a fresh 32-second shot clock. This is treated as a turnover.
6
Protecting against abuse: To prevent teams from exploiting players who cannot shoot from distance, the defensive team may only select the same player a maximum of 2 times per game as the challenge shooter, and never back-to-back.
7
Foul out rule: A player who reaches 6 fouls must sit out for 1 full possession (32 seconds). If the team has no eligible substitutes, the player sits the full possession before returning.

👨‍👩‍👧 Minor Participant Policy

1
Minimum age is 15. Players must be at least 15 years old to register and participate in HooperQuest32. Players under 15 will not be accepted regardless of skill level or parental permission.
2
Under-13 hard block. In compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), no personal data is collected from players under 13 years of age. Under-13 signups are blocked entirely.
3
Parental consent is mandatory for players aged 15–17. A parent or legal guardian must review and sign the HQ32 Minor Participant Consent & Waiver before the minor's registration is activated. The minor's registration is not complete until parental consent is received.
4
Parent/guardian contact required. A reachable parent or guardian phone number must be provided at registration. This number must be available during all game days in case of emergency.
5
Media consent for minors. Publishing video, photos, or highlights featuring a minor requires explicit written consent from a parent or guardian. This is collected as part of the parental consent form. Minors whose parents have not consented to media will not be featured in any published content.
6
Code of conduct applies fully. Minor participants are held to the same HQ32 Code of Conduct as adult players. No trash talk, no attitude, no fighting. Parents and guardians are encouraged to review the code of conduct with their child before game day.
7
The Keeper reserves the right to remove any minor participant from the league if their safety, wellbeing, or the safety of others is at risk — regardless of parental consent status.

🤝 Conduct & Sportsmanship

1
No trash talk. Period. HooperQuest32 is a zero-tolerance environment for trash talk. If your opponent makes a tough shot — acknowledge it, compliment it, and move on. Good energy only. We are here to compete and grow, not to tear each other down.
2
No attitude. Attitude, theatrics, and intimidation have no place here. Good sportsmanship is not optional — it is the standard. Celebrate your own plays with class, and respect your opponent's.
3
No fighting. Any physical altercation results in immediate removal from the game and potential suspension from HooperQuest32.
4
Honor the system. The foul system runs on the honor system. Call fouls honestly. Abusing foul calls disrespects the game and the community.
5
Accept decisions. Once a challenge outcome is determined (shot made or missed), the call is final. No further disputes on that possession.
6
Show up. If you sign up for a team, you commit to showing up. Notify your team as early as possible if you can't make it. No-shows without notice affect everyone.
7
Represent HQ32. On and off the court, you carry the HooperQuest32 name. Play hard. Play right. Grow every possession.
